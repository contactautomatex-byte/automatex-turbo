1) Strategy Checklist

  • Define your ICP: who you serve, pain points, desired outcomes.
  • Write a one-line offer: “I help X get Y without Z.”
  • List top 5 objections and prepare responses.
  • Pick one main channel (don’t spread too thin).

2) Content & Social Checklist

  • Choose 3 content angles: pain, proof, process.
  • Create 5 hooks per post and test the best.
  • Repurpose: blog → thread → carousel → email.
  • End with one CTA (not five).

3) SEO Checklist

  • Pick one cluster topic and create 3 supporting pages.
  • Write H1 + H2 outline based on search intent.
  • Add internal links to related pages (2–5).
  • Add FAQ and update content every 60–90 days.

4) Ads Checklist

  • Define one objective (lead, sale, call booking).
  • Create 3 angles: speed, savings, proof.
  • Generate 10 headline variations and test.
  • Track conversions with UTMs.

5) Funnels & Email Checklist

  • Create a lead magnet or “first step” offer.
  • Write a 5-email nurture sequence.
  • Include proof and objection handling.
  • Use follow-up logic (opened/clicked).

6) Tracking & Iteration Checklist

  • Use UTMs for every campaign link.
  • Track what gets clicks vs. what converts.
  • Double down on the top 20% assets.
  • Schedule refresh cycles for SEO pages.
