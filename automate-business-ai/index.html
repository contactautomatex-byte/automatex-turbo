1) Sales Automation

Automation starts with the pipeline. Most revenue is lost in follow-up and unclear proposals.

  • Follow-up sequences: 1 day / 3 days / 7 days.
  • Proposal templates with scope, timeline and deliverables.
  • Objection handling scripts (price, timing, trust).

2) Client Onboarding Workflow

A simple onboarding system prevents delays, confusion and scope creep.

  • Client intake form: goals, audience, assets, constraints.
  • Kickoff email template with expectations and timeline.
  • Asset checklist: brand, access, logins, previous data.
